Tribute to Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg, born on May 14, 1984, is an American computer programmer and Internet entrepreneur. He co-founded Facebook and serves as its chairman, chief executive officer, and controlling shareholder.

Mark Zuckerberg's impact on the tech industry has been profound. He transformed the way people connect and share information with the creation of Facebook, which has become one of the largest and most influential social media platforms in the world.

Beyond Facebook, Zuckerberg has been involved in various philanthropic efforts. In 2015, he and his wife, Priscilla Chan, pledged to donate 99% of their Facebook shares to the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, a charitable organization aimed at addressing global challenges.

Mark Zuckerberg's journey from a college student with a passion for coding to a key figure in the tech world showcases innovation, determination, and the ability to shape the future of communication.
